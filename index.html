About Hulk Hogan

Hulk Hogan is an entrepeneur and marketing genius. He has leveraged his understanding of marketing trends and consumer desire to earn millions of dollars in industries as far flung as shopping mall food courts and wrestling rings set up in shopping malls.

The "Hulkster," as he is known to his millions of "Hulkamaniacs" [fans] has also appeared in numerous Hollywood films, including Suburban Commando and Gremlins 2 (but only the theatrical release).
