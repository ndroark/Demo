About Hulk Hogan

Hulk Hogan is an entrepeneur and marketer. He has leveraged his understanding of market trends and consumer desires to earn millions of dollars in industries as far flung as shopping mall food courts and wrestling rings set up in shopping malls.

The "Hulkster," as he is known to his millions of "Hulkamaniacs" [fans] has also appeared in numerous Hollywood films, including Suburban Commando, Three Ninjas High Noon at Mega Mountain, and Gremlins 2 (but only the theatrical release).
